6. I never received any written discharge. At the expiration of each tour the company was discharged by verbal orders from the captain which is the way all of the militia were discharged. I always understood. 7. I am known to Jacob Lukens Grant, David Test, Joseph Jackson Isaac N Norris Isiah James Middleton Test and Dr. Chalkley James in my present neighborhood who can testify as to my character for veracity and their belief in my services as a soldier of the Revolution. John Hough Sworn and subscribed in open court the 18 day of June AD 1834. ] F. A. Raybold J P We Jacob Lukens Grant, Middleton Test and David Test are residing in the District of the Northern Liberties in the county of Philadelphia state aforesaid in the same neighborhood with the above named John House, hereby certify that we are well acquainted with the above named John House, that we believe him to be upward of seventy nine years of age, that we have been well acquainted with him for upwards of 15 years, that he is reputed and believed in the neighborhood where he resides to have been a soldier of the Revolution and we concur in that opinion. We also certify that there is no clergyman in the immediate vicinity of said John House & that he is a moral and pious man and sustains an impeachable character for veracity and truth and that entire confidence may be placed in his statements concerning his Revolutionary services. Middleton Test David Test Jacob Lukens Grant Affirmed and subscribed in open court June 18th 1834. F. A. Raybold
