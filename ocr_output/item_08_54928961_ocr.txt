6. I never received any written discharge. At the examination of each tour the company was discharged by verbal orders from the captain, which is the way all of the militia were discharged. I always understood I am known to Jacob Lukens Grant, David Test, Joseph Jackson, Isaac H. Norris, David Middleton, Test, and D. Phillip James in my present neighbourhood who can testify as to my character for veracity and their belief of my service as a soldier of the Revolution.

John Hough

Sworn and subscribed in open court the 18 day of June AD 1834.

F.A. Raybelle

The Jacob Lukens Grant, Middleton Test, and David Test be all residing in the District of the Northern Liberties in the county of Philadelphia state aforesaid, in the same neighbourhood with the above named John Hough, hereby certify that we are well acquainted with the above named John Hough, that we believe him to be upwards of seventy nine years of age, that we have been well acquainted with him for upwards of 15 years. That he is reputed and believed in the neighbourhood where he resides to have been a soldier of the Revolution, and we concur in that opinion. We also certify that there is no clergyman in the immediate vicinity of said John Hough &amp; that he is a moral and proud man, and sustains an unimpeachable character for veracity and truth, and that entire confidence may be placed in his statements concerning his Revolutionary service.

Affirmed and subscribed in open Court June 18th 1834

F.A. Raybelle

Middleton Test
David Test
J.L. Grant
