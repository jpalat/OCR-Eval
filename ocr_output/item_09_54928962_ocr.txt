And the said court do hereby declare their opinion
after the investigation of the matter and after putting
the interrogatories prescribed by the War Department
that the above named applicant was a Revolutionary
soldier and served as he states, and the court
further certify that it appears to them that
Larkens Grant, and David Test, who have signed
the preceding certificate are persons of good standing
and character, are persons of credit
and their statement entitled to full confidence.
They also certify that said Jacob Cuthers Green &amp; David Teste
reside in the immediate vicinity of
said applicant, and that there is no clergyman
residing in this immediate vicinity who is acquainted with the applicant
revolutionary service.

Edward King, President Judge

Herbert P. Pinnell
