31 SELVIDGE STREET
DALTON, GEORGIA
Nov 4th 22
PENSION OFFICE
NOV 6 1922

Pension Bureau
Washington, D.C.

Gentlemen,

Will you please tell me if you have
a Revolutionary record of the following
men, two at least of whom were recommended
for a "county." Of course I know this does not
necessarily mean a pension, but I am
hoping you have some Rev. War record.

Jacob Miller, of Bedford Co. Va. (Franklin)
John or James Huff, Loudon Co. Va.
Richard Abbott, Culpepper Co. Va.
Thomas West of Va. or Tenn. (Married a Hamilton)
Thanking you for the courtesy, I am
Very truly,
Mattie Lee Huff
