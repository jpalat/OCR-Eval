Date of application for pension, June 18, 1834.  His cl. was al.
Residence at date of application, Northern Liberties, Phila. Co. Penn.
Age at date of application, b. Mch. 12, 1755 in Warminster Twp. Bucks Co. Penn.
Remarks:  Moved to Loudoun Co. Va. in 1778 where he resided until 1782.  He refers to his sister-in-law Miss Humphreys as possessing the family bible.  There are no other family data.
